Common questions

How do I add a card?

Open the Scan tab, scan your barcode (or enter manually), add the store name, and save.

Can I use the app without internet?

Yes. Loyalty Cache works offline.

Is there a limit to how many cards I can save?

No. There are no card limits.

Does the app show ads?

No. Loyalty Cache has no ads.

How do I enable or disable iCloud backup?

Open Settings in the app and toggle iCloud Backup.

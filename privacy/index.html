Loyalty Cache Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Loyalty Cache is designed to be privacy-first.

Data storage

Your card data is stored locally on your device by default.

If you enable iCloud Backup, your card data is synced to your private iCloud account so it can be available across your Apple devices.

Data we collect

Loyalty Cache does not use third-party ads and does not sell your data.

Loyalty Cache does not require account creation inside the app.

Camera access

The camera is used only to scan barcodes when you choose to scan a card.

Third-party services

When iCloud Backup is enabled, Apple iCloud services process your data according to Apple’s terms and policies.

Children’s privacy

Loyalty Cache is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact loyaltycache@icloud.com.
